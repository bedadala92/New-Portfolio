Background:

Kayla Coleman is a community organizer at the Civic Media Center in Gainesville, Florida. She is fighting for the rights of the underrepresented and marginalized in the community. I worked with Kayla to tell her story to the world and to introduce new members in the community to the Civic Media Center. Originally an audio-visual story, I decided to put together a website and share it with the world.

Both Kayla and her story are an inspiration.
